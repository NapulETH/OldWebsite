Announcement!

            
                
                    We are very proud to announce that the second edition of spaghett-eth will be hosted in Napoli! At the moment, we are collaborating with the broader spaghett-eth team and we are doing our best to offer you the best possible experience in our city. Here are some useful info:
                    
    
                        
  • Official event link
    • 
                        
  • Where: Napoli, hosted at the Fondazione Foqus
    • 
                        
  • When: May 24th-26th 2023
    • 
                        
  • Theme: raWstories, that is, tales from the crypto-trenches
    • 
                        
  • You can apply as a: 
                            
      
                                
    • Speaker, if you want to give a talk. Rough submission deadlines can be found here.
      • 
                                
    • Volounteer, if you want to help us with running the event. You'll get a free ticket!
      • 
                                
    • Partner, if you want to spread the word about your project while helping us either with funding or with in-kind contributions.
      •
